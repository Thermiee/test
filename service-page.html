Our Services

Our service offering in the real estate sector of the economy cuts across the following:

Real Estate Development (Build-Operate-Transfer (BOT)

Under the BOT, we identify lands or dilapidated buildings in commercially viable locations and partner with the owners of such lands by entering into a long-term partnership/contract with them where we finance, design, construct, own and operate a facility stated in the lease/contract. This enables us to recover our investment, generate good returns, operate and maintain expenses in the project. During the life of the lease, we hand over some units of apartment to the land-owners without any interference whatsoever on the remaining units, to enable them generate sustainable income pending the expiration of the lease that the whole property will revert to them.

Joint Ventures (JV) Development

Under this business arrangement, we enter into a project arrangement with landowners, who provide land in commercially viable areas and we provide funding for the construction of a building or buildings and thereafter jointly own the building(s) forever.

Buy, Design, Build and Sell

We basically purchase commercial viable land in good location and construct single and multi-family unit apartments (flats and duplexes) and thereafter sell to Nigerians within the country and Diaspora who desire to own their own properties without having to build by themselves.

Project Management & Supervision

We initiate, plan, execute, control and close real estate projects within a defined budget and time-frame for our clients without the clients having to worry about cost overrun. We also supervise existing projects for clients to ensure that such projects are completed within an allotted time frame and also within budget.
