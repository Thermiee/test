About Us

REASON FOR OUR EXISTENCE

Dinero Cities and Structures Limited (DCSL) was registered in August 2017 under the Company and Allied Matters Act (CAMA) of 1990 as a Limited Liability Company.

DCSL is engaged in the business of Property Development & Management, Real Estate Investments, Project Management and we also play in the hospitality business as we provide world class, luxurious and affordable short-let accommodation.

The business idea was borne out of the need to be a part of the solution to the country’s current housing deficit of 20 million units.

We are quite aware as a business that opportunities to generating consistent profits abound where challenges abound. Our business is designed as a solution to meeting this need through the provision of single housing, multifamily housing and hostels, particularly for the ever increasing middle class/work force and students population whose focus is proximity to their offices and campuses.

We also employ sound and effective management principles to ensuring that we turn consistent profits on properties we manage both for the organization and all stakeholders.
