OUR TEAM

Femi Bakare

President/CEO

Femi Bakare is the President/Chief Executive of Dinero Cities and Structures Limited (DCSL), a fast growing Real Estate Development and Project Management Company based in Lagos, Nigeria.

He is a seasoned investment analyst and project manager with close to two decades experience in different sectors of the economy. He is responsible for driving the overall vision, growth and strategic plan of the organization. 

Femi is passionate about teaching investments and entrepreneurship especially to younger generation, who he believes are the key to unlocking the wealth of our nation.

Dapo Adeyemi

Chief Technical Officer

Dapo Adeyemi is the Chief Technical Officer of DCSL. He holds an electrical engineering certificate from Moshood Abiola Polytechnic. He is also an industrial & production engineering graduate of the University of Ibadan.

He started his working career as an electrical engineer with Hambaj Properties Limited and became a manager within a short period, overseeing the property management arm of the organization.

He is currently the Chief Technical Officer of the organization, coordinating all structural and electrical work.

Faith Bakare

Vice President, Property Management/HR

Faith Bakare, a graduate of public Administration from Nasarawa State University.

She has over a decade experience in the corporate world where she was responsible for different Admin, HR and related functions

Faith is the Vice President in charge of Property Management, HR & Admin matters of DCSL.

Adewale Kudaisi

Project Manager

Adewale is coordinating supervisor of all projects handled by the organization.

He is an accountant by training but his passion for real estate made him rise fast in the organization within a short period of time as he bring new ideas and energy into management and supervision of different projects he has handled.

Adewale also oversees the marketing and public relation functions of the organization.
