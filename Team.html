OUR TEAM

Femi Bakare

President/CEO

Femi Bakare is the President/Chief Executive of Dinero Cities and Structures Limited (DCSL), a fast growing Real Estate Development and Project Management Company based in Lagos, Nigeria.

He is a seasoned investment analyst and project manager with close to two decades experience in different sectors of the economy. He is responsible for driving the overall vision, growth and strategic plan of the organization. 

Femi is passionate about teaching investments and entrepreneurship especially to younger generation, who he believes are the key to unlocking the wealth of our nation.

Dapo Adeyemi

CTO

Dapo Adeyemi is the Chief Technical Officer of DCSL. He holds an electrical engineering certificate from Moshood Abiola Polytechnic.

He is also an industrial and production engineering graduate of the University of Ibadan.  Dapo started his working career as an electrical engineer with Hambaj Properties Limited where he oversees all electrical and mechanical works.He rose to become a manager within a short period and oversees the property management arm of the organization for close to a decade before venturing into property development within Lagos metropolis. 

He is currently the Chief Technical Officer of the organization, coordinating all structural and electrical work.

Faith Bakare

She is the Vice President in charge of Property Management, HR & Admin matter of DCSL.
